When it comes to the web industry (or computer industry for that matter) men generally rule the roost. In 2007 <a href="http://www.alistapart.com/articles/2007surveyresults/">A List Apart published their web design survey results</a> and found that out of nearly 33,000 professionals who participated, <strong>82.8% were male</strong> while only <strong>16.1% were female</strong> (1.1% gave no answer).

<a href="http://www.leemunroe.com/25-hot-female-web-designers/"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-400" title="Female Web Designers" src="http://www.leemunroe.com/wp-content/uploads/nails.jpg" alt="Female Web Designers" /></a>

However, as I browse the web today I'm finding more and more inspirational and creative websites from females across the globe. Just because there are more men involved in the web community doesn't mean that the best stuff comes from us. There's a certain <strong>elegance, beauty and tone</strong> that the female touch can add to a design.

I've compiled a list of <del datetime="2008-10-27T16:56:08+00:00">25</del> 26 female web designers who I believe are hot at web design and whose designs are an inspiration to the whole of the web design community.
